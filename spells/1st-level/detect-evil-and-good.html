Khelvoren

Detect Evil and Good

1st-Level Divination Spell

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: 30ft

Components: V, S

Duration: Concentration, up to 10 minutes

For the duration, you know if there is an aberration, celestial, demons, devils, elemental, fey, or undead within range of you, as well as where the creature is located. Similarly, you know if there is a place or object within range of you that has been magically consecrated or desecrated.

The spell can penetrate most barriers, but it is blocked by 1 foot of stone, 1 inch of common metal, a thin sheet of lead, or 3 feet of wood or dirt.

Spell Lists: Cleric, Paladin
