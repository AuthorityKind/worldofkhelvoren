Khelvoren

Electrify

Credits: KibblesTasty

1st-Level Evocation Spell

Casting Time: 1 Bonus Action

Range: Self

Components: V, S

Duration: 1 Round

You channel Lighting Into your hands. After casting this spell, if you make a successful melee spell or weapon attack, the target takes an additional 1d8 lightning damage and must make a constitution Saving throw. On a failed save, the target becomes stunned until the start of their next turn. The spell ends early after it has dealt damage.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, if the target fails the Constitution Saving throw, they instead become stunned until the end of their next turn.

Spell Lists: Magus, Ranger, Sorcerer, Wizard
