Khelvoren

Arcane Fist

1st-Level Evocation Spell

Casting Time: 1 Bonus Action

Range: Self

Components: V, S

Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute

You channel your magic into your body as if it were Ki. While under the effects of this spell, you use your Spellcasting Modifier for your unarmed attacks, and your damage dice for your unarmed attacks become a 1d8 unless you have another unarmed damage dice to choose from that is higher. Your unarmed attacks also count as magical for the purpose of overcoming resistances

At Higher Levels: Casting this spell at 2nd level or higher grants a bonus to your attack and damage rolls with unarmed attacks equal to half of the spell level of spell slot used for this spell (rounding up). If you already have a bonus to both your attack and damage rolls with unarmed attacks from some other effect, you instead only gain the highest of the two bonuses. Addtionally, at 5th level, the spell no longer requires concentration.

Spell Lists: Magus, Sorcerer, Wizard
