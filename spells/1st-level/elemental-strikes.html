Khelvoren

Elemental Strikes

Credits: KibblesTasty

1st-Level Transmutation Spell

Casting Time: 1 Bonus Action

Range: Self

Components: V, M (a melee weapon you are proficient)

Duration: 1 Round

You touch a weapon in your possession and imbue it with elemental energy, choosing from Acid, Cold, Fire, Lightning, Poison, or Thunder. Until the start of your next turn, attack with that weapon deals 1d6 damage of the elemental damage type chosen. If you touch a weapon with the light property, you can make 1 weapon attack as part of the action spent to cast it.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a slot of 2nd level or higher, the spell lasts an additional round for each spell level above the 1st.

Spell Lists: Magus, Ranger
