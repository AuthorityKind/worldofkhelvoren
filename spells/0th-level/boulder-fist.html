Khelvoren

Boulder Fist

Transmutation Cantrip

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: Touch

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

Your arm grows massive rocks and stones on until your entire arm is a larger boulder-like structure, and you then throw a striking punch. You make a melee spell attack roll against a single target. If you hit, you deal 1d10 bludgeoning damage. The creature also has to make a strength save against your spell DC. If they fail, they are knocked prone.
