Khelvoren

Shilleglagh

Transmutation Cantrip

Casting Time: 1 Bonus Action

Range: Touch

Components:V, S, M (mistletoe, a shamrock leaf, and a club or quarterstaff)

Duration: 1 minute

The wood of a club or quarterstaff you are holding is imbued with nature’s power. For the duration, you can use your spellcasting ability instead of Strength for the attack and damage rolls of melee attacks using that weapon, and the weapon’s damage die becomes a d8. If the weapon has the Versatile propperty, then it's damage die becomes 2d4 when its wielded twohanded. The weapon also becomes magical, if it isn’t already. The spell ends if you cast it again or if you let go of the weapon.

Spell Lists: Druid
