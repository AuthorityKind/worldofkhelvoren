Khelvoren

Sword Burst

Conjuration Cantrip

Casting Time: 1 action

Range: 5ft

Components:V

Duration: Instantaneous

You create a momentary circle of spectral blades that sweep around you. All other creatures within the spell's range of you must succeed on a Dexterity saving throw or take 1d6 force damage.

At Higher Levels:This spell's damage increases by 1d6 when you reach 5th level (2d6), 11th level (3d6), and 17th level (4d6).

Spell Lists: Artificer, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
