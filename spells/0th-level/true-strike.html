Khelvoren

True Strike

Divination Cantrip

Casting Time: 1 action

Range: Self

Components:S

Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute

You glimpse short visions of the future, seeing your enemies' weaknesses. Within the duration of the spell, you may reroll one attack roll once, where the spell then ends.

At Higher Levels:At 5th level, you may reroll 2 attack rolls before the spell ends, and at 11th level, you may reroll 3 attack rolls and at 17th level, you may reroll 4 attack rolls. You can only reroll an attack roll with this spell more than once.

Spell Lists: Bard, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
