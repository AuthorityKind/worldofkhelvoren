Legionnaire

Original Subclass Credit to u/unclecaveman1

The Legionnaires are heavily armored defensive masters, fighting in tight formations alongside their shield brothers and protecting one another with their massive tower shields. Often originating as soldiers in professional armies, mercenary companies, or even bodyguards for nobles and royalty, Legionnaires make excellent adventurers as they find themselves as the backbone of any party, bolstering their allies and driving them to keep up the fight, shielding them from harm, and holding the line of battle so nothing can pass. Some even become so experienced with their polished shields that they find they can deflect some spells back at their attackers.

Tortoise Formation

At 3rd level, while wielding a shield you can use a bonus action to assume a defensive stance. Your speed drops to 0, your AC increases by 1, and ranged attacks against you are made at a disadvantage. This bonus lasts until the beginning of your next turn. You may not activate this feature if you have moved more than half your speed during the current round

Standard Bearer

Staring at 7th level you are a beacon of courage on the battlefield, and your allies are emboldened knowing you are fighting beside them. You may shout words of encouragement, wave a banner or standard, or let out a war-cry, reminding your companions what they are fighting for. As a bonus action, you and all conscious allies within 30 feet of you that can see you gain temporary HP equal to half your Fighter level. You can only do this once and need to take a short or long rest to be able to do it again. At 15th level, the temporary HP is instead equal to your Fighter level.

Brothers in Arms

Starting at 10th level, when an ally adjacent to you is attacked you may use your reaction to replace their AC with your own. If the attack still hits your ally, then you regain your expended reaction.

Hold Steady

Starting at 15th level, when you use Tortoise Formation on your turn, all terrain in a 10ft radius of you is considered difficult terrain for your enemies until the start of your next turn.

Bulwark

When your shield is raised, you stand nearly untouchable. At 18th level, when your Tortoise Formation is active, your AC bonus from it becomes +2, you have advantage on Strength and Constitution Saves, and you have advantage on, and add a + 4 to, Dexterity Saves made to determine whether you take half of full damage, like from a dragons breath. Additionally, activating your Tortoise Formation no longer requires a Bonus Action and you can activate at any time on your turn. You may also have it activate when you roll initiave.
