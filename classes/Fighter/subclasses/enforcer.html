Fighter Subclass

Enforcer

Original Subclass Credit to Aeron

Bonus Proficiencies

When you choose this archetype at 3rd level, you gain proficiency in either Athletics or Intimidation. You also gain proficiency in one gaming set.

Crowd Control

Starting at 3rd level, you can use a bonus action on your turn to attempt a grapple or shove a creature. Additionally, you gain the following benefits:

● When you roll your supperiority die, that is a part of an attack or action made against a creature that you have grappled, you roll the die twice and choose the higher result.

● You add your Constitution Modifier to Athletics checks made to grapple or maintain grapples.

Powerful Stature

At 7th level, you count as one size larger for the purpose of grappling, shoving or moving a grappled creature.

Cheap Shot

Also, when you reach 7th level, whenever you hit a creature that is grappled by you with a melee weapon attack you deal an extra 1d6.

Intimidation Tactics

At 10th level, when you roll for initiative, you can choose any number of creatures within 60ft of you that you can see. If the creature can see and hear you, they must make a Wisdom check contested by your Intimidation check. If they fail, the first attack roll or ability check they perform in the first round has a 1d4 penalty to that roll.

Tough as Nails

Starting at 15th level, you have advantage on saving throws against being knocked prone, moved, restrained or stunned.

Brutal Grip

Starting at 18th level, creatures have disadvantage on Athletics and Acrobatics check made to escape your grapple. Additionally, if an effect that forcibly moves either you or a creature that you are grappling that removes a creature from your reach, you can choose to maintain the grapple. If you do so the grappled creature stays within your reach and both you and the creature are moved by the effect.
