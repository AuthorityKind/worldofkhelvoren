Fighter Subclass

Champion

Martial Prodigy

When you choose this subclass, your natural mastery of battle begins to blossom. You gain an additional Superiority Die. When you reach 10th level, this increases to two additional Superiority Dice, and when you reach 18th level, this increases to three additional Superiority Dice instead.

Masterful Flurry

Also when you choose this subclass, you can for a brief moment call upon your most peerless training. When you use your Action Surge feature, and the extra action on an Attack action, you may use a Maneuver you know as part of the attacks of that Action, without having to expend a Superiority Die.

Observant Combatant

Lastly, also when you choose this subclass, you have the ability to get a read on your opponent simply by battling them. When you have hit a weapon attack against a creature or a creature have hit you with a weapon attack, you can use your reaction to use your Know Your Enemy feature, targeting that creature.

Additional Fighting Style

At 7th level, the techniques at your disposal deepens even greater. You gain an additional Fighting Style, that you don't already have, that is available to the Fighter.

Unconquerable

At 10th level, you seem to show unwavering fortitude in the moments you over overcome adversity. When you use your Indomitable feature, you roll your Superiority Die twice and choose either roll. Additionally, after you have succeeded in a Saving Throw where you used your Indomitable feature, you then also gain advantage on all saving throws until the end of your next turn.

Adaptive Style

At 15th level, the rhythms of the fight are so innate to you, that you can alter you way of fighting to suit your needs. When you take a long rest, you can choose to replace a number of Maneuvers equal to half your Strength or Dexterity Modifier (your choice), rounding up, that you know with a different Maneuver. Additionally, as a Bonus Action, you can replace a Maneuver you know with a different Maneuver. You need to take a long rest to be able to do this again.

Warrior of Myth

At 18th level, the tales of your battles are more reminiscent of old legends than anything else. You gain the following benefits:

● When you are reduced to 0HP by a creature, you can choose to defy death. Your HP is set 1, you gain a number of Temporary HP equal to your Fighter Level + your Constitution Score, which last till you take a short or long rest, and you regain all expended Superiority Dice and uses of Action Surge.

● When you use your Action Surge feature, you also gain Temporary HP equal to your Superiority Dice + your Constitution Score, which last for 1 minute. If you already have Temporary HP, you can choose to instead regain HP equal to the Temporary HP you would have been given from this ability.

● Creatures cannot flank you.
