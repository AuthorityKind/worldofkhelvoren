Fighter Subclass

Temporal Drifter

Lesser Precognition

When you choose this archetype, you then gain the ability to, for a very brief instant, see the moments to come. You can expend 1 Superiority Dice and use your reaction to see 6 seconds into the future. The DM describes what the most likely scenario is. Your character does have agency in this vision, so can choose to interact with the environment, like opening a door or saying something to someone. Once the vision ends, you return to the present with the knowledge of that vision.

Temporal Insight

Additionally at 3rd level. You get a greater instinctive gut feeling of what's to come. You add your Intelligence modifier to your initiative.

Borrowed Time

At 7th level, you can pocket your own time to then use it later, slowing you down now but speeding you up later. You can expend 1 Superiority Dice to use an action to get an additional action on your next turn. You can only do this once per turn.

Chronosurge

Also at 7th You can speed up time for yourself for a brief moment. When you use your Action Surge feature, all of your speeds are increased equal to a number of feet equal to 10 times your Intelligence modifier until the end of your turn.

Redo

At 10th level, you can spin back time for yourself to give yourself a second chance. As a reaction, you can expend 2 Superiority Dice to give back all expended actions, bonus actions, and features, and you regain all of your spend movement that you have spent this turn. All of the effects of your previously taken actions, bonus actions, or features become reverted and nullified and you get placed back where you started. You keep any knowledge you acquired and all experiences you had.

Timely Intuition

Additionally, at 10th level, you have acquired a deeper source of time manipulation and foresight. You gain a pool of Temporal Points equal to your Intelligence modifier (minimum of 1). These Temporal Points can be spent in place of Superiority Dice for Temporal Drifter Features. You regain all expended Temporal Points after a long rest.

Greater Precognition

At 15th level, you can see further into the future than you could before. As an action, you see 1 minute into the future. This ability functions identically to Lesser Precognition except for the duration of the vision. Once you do this, you need to take a long rest to do it again or expend 2 Superiority Dice.

Recall

Also at 15th level, you learn to make you jump back in time while the world stays put. As a bonus action, you can expend 2 Superiority Dice to teleport back where you were 6 seconds ago, or where you started your last turn if your are in combat. You can choose to expend additional dice beyond the cost of this ability. For each 2 extra Superiority Dice you expend above the normal cost, you jump an additional 6 seconds back in time, or go back another turn to where you started on that turn you went back to if you are in combat.

Timely Intuition

At 18th level, you can let the flow of time go through you, allowing you unprecedented foresight. As a bonus action, you enter your Temporal Exploitation, which lasts a number of rounds equal to your Intelligence Modifier. While in your Temporal Exploitation, you gain the following benefits:

• You gain advantage on all weapon attack rolls and saving throws. You can choose to reroll the lowest of the 2 dice, made as part of a weapon attack roll or saving throw, once per Temporal Exploitation.

• Creatures have disadvantage on opportunity attacks against you. You can choose to make them reroll the highest of the 2 dice, made as part of the opportunity attack roll, once per Temporal Exploitation.

• You get a +3 bonus to your AC.
